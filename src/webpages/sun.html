Sun

The Source of Life.

The sun is a star and is extremely important for life on Earth. The sun provides us with energy, which brings life on our planet. It defines the seasons, the harvests, and even the sleep patterns of all living creatures on Earth.

Sun is the closest star to our planet. The Earth orbits around the sun. The sun is way bigger than the Earth.It's hot!! The sun's average surface temperature is more than 10,000 degrees Fahrenheit!!

Interesting facts about Earth

About

Our home planet Earth is a rocky, terrestrial planet. It has a solid and active surface with mountains, valleys, canyons, plains and so much more. Earth is special because it is an ocean planet. Water covers 70% of Earth's surface.

Earth's atmosphere is made mostly of nitrogen and has plenty of oxygen for us to breathe. The atmosphere also protects us from incoming meteoroids, most of which break up before they can hit the surface.
